About Shri Satadhar
Precast

Shri Satadhar Precast is a trusted name in Gujarat's precast manufacturing industry, delivering superior-quality concrete products that stand the test of time. Based in Gondal, we specialise in producing RCC boundary walls, chain link fencing, RCC piling, MMS foundations, and custom concrete solutions for solar and infrastructure projects.

Led by Mr. Jaymin Patel, we combine field expertise with customer-focused service. Whether it's a single unit or bulk delivery, we ensure timely execution, competitive pricing, and on-site support.
